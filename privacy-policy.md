# FitLokma Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 31, 2026

FitLokma is a nutrition and calorie tracking application.

## Information We Collect

- Meal information entered by users
- Nutrition values
- Water intake records
- Step count data (with permission)
- Food photos selected by users

## HealthKit

FitLokma may access HealthKit step count data if the user grants permission.

Health data is never sold or shared with advertisers.

## Camera and Photos

FitLokma may access the camera and photo library to analyze food images.

## Data Sharing

FitLokma does not sell personal data to third parties.
